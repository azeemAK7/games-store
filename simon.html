SIMON GAME

RULES

1.Remember and repeat a sequence of colors and sounds generated by the Simon device

2.Start with a single color, then repeat and extend the sequence as the game progresses.

3.Press the buttons quickly and accurately to match the growing sequence.

4.Make a mistake by pressing the wrong button or failing to repeat the correct sequence, and the game ends

5.Aim to reach the longest possible sequence and challenge your memory and reaction time in this classic electronic memory game.
